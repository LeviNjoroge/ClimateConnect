Introduction to Climate Change

Introduction

Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time, with far-reaching implications for the environment, economies, and societies worldwide. To address this issue effectively, it is essential to understand its definition, causes, and global significance.

Understanding Climate Change: Definition, Causes, and Global Significance

Definition of Climate Change

Climate change refers to significant and long-term changes in the Earth's climate, particularly shifts in temperature, precipitation patterns, and weather events. While climate variability is a natural phenomenon, the term "climate change" is now predominantly used to describe the rapid and unprecedented changes driven by human activities since the Industrial Revolution. These changes are altering the planet's ecosystems and threatening the stability of natural and human systems.

UNFCCC Definition

The United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) defines climate change as "a change of climate which is attributed directly or indirectly to human activity that alters the composition of the global atmosphere and which is in addition to natural climate variability observed over comparable time periods."
